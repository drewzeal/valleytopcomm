Our Products

Valutok Platform

Valutok https://valutok.com is a Social Networking platform that offers multiple content monetization rewards to its users for sharing content and other social activities. Like typical social networking sites you can add friends, chat privately, follow and be followed back,but on Valutok your activity counts as points and are recorded and converted to financial bonuses as a way of rewarding loyalty. Every user has a wallet in which bonuses are stored and cashed out in user's specific currencies at any point in time.
